FAQ

How many team members can I invite?

You can invite a maximum of two team members. Any number
above two will incur a fee of $15 per person.

What is the maximum file upload size?

No more than 2GB. All files in your account must fit your
alloted storage space.

How do I reset my password?

You just click on the Reset Password link, enter your
username and you will be sent an email with futher insructions
to reset your password.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, definitely. You can cancel your subscription for
the normal package at any time but there is a small cancellation fee
for the other packages.

Do you provide additional support?

Of course. We have an online virtual assistant available
24/7. There is live support during office hours at each of our locations.
